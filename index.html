Program Overview

UNITEN ROBOTICS 2017 - will host a Combat Robot Competition.

Two manual/remote-controlled robots are placed in a ring. The robots try to avoid falling out or avoid being pushed out by the opponent robot. The first robot that get outside of the ring loses the game.

Mobile Robotic Club (MRC)

Mobile Robotics Club (mrc) is the official robotics club in uniten. our members consists of uniten's student from various fields of engineering.we have received support from the management of the university and from others organization. mrc has participated in several robotic competitions both local and international and have attained a good placing in most of the competitions. some of the recent competition that we have participate are malaysia robotic competition (mrc), iium robotic competition (irc), fighting robot competition (frc) and utar inter campus robotic competition.

IEEE PES UNITEN SB

IEEE Power & Energy Society (pes) uniten student branch (sb) is a student branch chapter has been established in february 2016. the aim of this society is to make uniten visible on the international level, especially in ieee pes. ieee pes is a worldwide, non-profit association engaged in electric power engineering. ieee pes uniten sb are focusing more on power and energy field. however, it has a lot of activities involving the community. by becoming an ieee member, it enables for an international networking with ieee members around the world.
